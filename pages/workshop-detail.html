About this activity 活動詳情

Step into the glowing world of neon at our Central PMQ studio. In this 2-hour immersive workshop, you’ll design and build your own neon-style sign using modern LED flex tubing — safe, cool to the touch, and just as vibrant as traditional glass neon.

We start with a brief intro to neon history and design principles. Then you’ll sketch your idea, choose your colours, and learn how to bend, cut, and mount the tubing onto an acrylic backing. Our instructors guide you every step of the way.

By the end of the session, you’ll walk out with a finished piece — your name, a favourite quote, a symbol, or anything you can imagine — glowing in colours you picked yourself.
